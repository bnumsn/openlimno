# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

OpenLimno adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Scientific integrity addendum

OpenLimno is a scientific tool. We additionally require:

- **No fabricated data.** Sample data, HSI curves, validation cases must trace to a `survey_campaign` or `paper_doi`.
- **No undisclosed conflicts of interest.** If you contribute species parameters drawn from work funded by a hydropower or fisheries operator, disclose in PR description.
- **No selective citation.** Methodological criticism (e.g., HSI independence assumption, FishXing attraction omission) must be acknowledged where relevant.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the Project Steering Committee (PSC) at conduct@openlimno.org (placeholder). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The PSC will follow the [Contributor Covenant Enforcement Ladder](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines):

1. Correction (private warning)
2. Warning (consequences for continued behavior)
3. Temporary ban
4. Permanent ban
